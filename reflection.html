Group Reflection

Team #IDK-5 as a collective, was a fantastic learning experience. From completing the assignment to assisting each other, where skillsets faced challenges, and learning opportunities arose, it was a tremendous experience for all involved. A few of us have already started to reach out to each other in other media platforms, such as Linkedin.

While the experience was enjoyable, it did come with its fair share of trials and tribulations. Early and midway throughout the assignment, Team #IDK-5 had lost two of its members, requiring that the workload be re-assigned. In light of this new workload, we knew we needed to be dynamic and discussed together that the best approach was to have individually assigned responsibilities for segmented tasks.

What we found quite surprising was how proactive we all were when work needed to be re-assigned or collaborated on and had been done with non-existent resistance. Many of us have said that they had worked in other group work where the desired result had not occurred, and communication within the team had broken down.

The frequent feedback provided amongst team members was how proud each of us was of the quality of the project had been delivered and of each other's willingness to get this completed.

We found in particular that despite all being separate personality types, traits and backgrounds, we all worked quite well, despite the initial conversation and feedback from the Team Profile at the start of the project.

In hindsight, we are all looking forward to the next opportunity that we get to collaborate again with each other.
